## Introduction

### Top 5 Concrete Improvements:

1.  **Kill the Roadmap (p. 4):** Delete the "Section 2 provides..." paragraph. Your headers are clear; the reader doesn't need a table of contents in prose form.
2.  **Narrativize the Theory (p. 5-6):** Convert the bullet points in Section 2.3 and 3.4 into prose. Use active verbs. "Laws didn't just protect managers; they choked off the entry of new rivals."
